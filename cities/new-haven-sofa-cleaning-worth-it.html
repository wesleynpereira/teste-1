Is Professional Sofa Cleaning Worth It for New Haven, CT Homeowners?

Yes, professional sofa cleaning is worth it for most New Haven households, especially homes with kids, pets, or sofas that haven't been deep cleaned in over a year. It removes embedded dirt, allergens, and odor sources that vacuuming alone can't reach.

What Professional Cleaning Removes That DIY Can't

  • Embedded dirt and skin oils built up in high-contact areas like armrests
  • Dust mites and allergens trapped deep in cushion fill
  • Odor sources from pets or daily use that sprays only mask
  • Old, set-in stains that need fabric-specific treatment

How Long Results Last in New Haven Homes

Most households across New Haven and New Haven County see noticeably fresher upholstery for 12 to 18 months after a professional cleaning, sooner for homes with pets or frequent guests.

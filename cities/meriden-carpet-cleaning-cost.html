Carpet Cleaning Cost in Meriden, CT: What Local Homeowners Pay

Carpet cleaning in Meriden, CT typically costs between $30 and $80 per room, or roughly $0.25 to $0.45 per square foot, depending on carpet condition and whether pet odor or deep stain treatment is needed. Most Meriden homes spend between $200 and $400 for a full carpet cleaning visit covering several rooms.

Homes across Meriden and the rest of New Haven County vary widely in carpet age and fiber type, which is one of the biggest factors in pricing. Older, denser carpet generally needs more pre-treatment time than newer synthetic blends.

What Local Factors Affect Pricing in Meriden

  • Number of rooms and total square footage
  • Carpet condition and how long it's been since the last deep cleaning
  • Stairs and hallways, often priced separately from open rooms
  • Add-on treatments like pet odor removal or stain-specific spot treatment

How Much Does It Cost to Clean 1,000 Sq Ft of Carpet?

Cleaning 1,000 square feet of carpet typically costs between $250 and $450, depending on carpet condition, the number of rooms involved, and whether stain treatment or pet odor removal is needed on top of standard carpet cleaning. That works out to roughly $0.25 to $0.45 per square foot for most homes in Fairfield County and New Haven County.

What Affects the Price Per Square Foot

Carpet cleaning pricing isn't one-size-fits-all, and a quote that ignores these factors usually isn't accurate once a technician actually sees the home:

  • Carpet condition — heavily soiled carpet with deep traffic lanes takes more passes and pre-treatment than lightly used carpet
  • Furniture moving — rooms that need furniture moved before cleaning add time
  • Stairs and hallways — often priced separately from open square footage
  • Add-on treatments — pet odor treatment, deep stain removal, or carpet protectant push the price above the base rate
  • Carpet type — thicker, higher-pile carpet absorbs more cleaning solution and takes longer to dry

Why "Cheap" Carpet Cleaning Quotes Can Cost More Later

A rock-bottom price per square foot often means a single quick pass with minimal pre-treatment, which leaves embedded dirt behind and can mean booking a second cleaning sooner than expected. A fair carpet cleaning cost reflects a full process: pre-inspection, fiber-safe pre-treatment, deep extraction, and a fast-dry finish, not just a quick surface rinse.

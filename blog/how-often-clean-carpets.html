How Often Should You Get Your Carpets Professionally Cleaned?

Carpet looks clean long before it actually is. Dust, allergens, and bacteria settle deep into the fibers long before stains or odors show up on the surface, which is why most cleaning professionals recommend a professional carpet cleaning every 6 to 12 months.

If your Connecticut home sees daily traffic from kids, pets, or a busy household, lean toward the 6-month mark. Carpets in formal living rooms or guest bedrooms with light use can often stretch closer to a full year between cleanings.

A few signs it's time to book sooner rather than later:

  • Traffic lanes are visibly darker than the rest of the carpet
  • A musty or stale smell lingers even after vacuuming
  • Anyone in the home has started noticing more allergy symptoms indoors

Homeowners in Danbury, Stamford, and Norwalk often pair a routine carpet cleaning with upholstery cleaning or area rug cleaning in the same visit to refresh every soft surface in the home at once.

Sectional and Sofa Cleaning Tips Between Professional Visits

A sectional sees more daily use than almost any other piece of furniture in the house, which means a little upkeep between professional visits goes a long way.

  • Vacuum cushions and crevices weekly to stop dirt from working into the fabric
  • Rotate and flip removable cushions so wear spreads evenly
  • Blot spills immediately with a clean cloth instead of letting them set
  • Keep pets off freshly treated areas until fully dry

Even with regular upkeep, fabric eventually needs a deeper clean than a vacuum and a damp cloth can provide. Professional sectional and sofa cleaning treats every cushion individually, including removable covers, instead of a single quick pass over the surface.

Families in Trumbull, Shelton, and Stratford typically schedule a deeper cleaning every 12 to 18 months, sooner for households with pets or young kids. BluePeak Cleaning Co. also offers pet odor treatment for sectionals that need more than a standard cleaning.
